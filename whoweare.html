Who We Are

Erin Lepone

Erin is a co-founder of the Lady Savant Foundation. Before getting into the philanthropic world, she had a career in finance where she worked for an alternative asset firm investing in hedge funds for close to 8 years. As fate would have it, she met her husband working in the same field and now is a stay at home mom in NJ with him and their 2 beautiful and rambunctious young boys, as well as very gifted and talented step son teenager.

After leaving work to raise the boys, she ventured out in the non-profit world and LOVED it! She was lucky enough to start working with The Robin Hood Foundation, a NYC based organization. Erin assisted in various projects over the past 2 years there including outreach programs such as the Lemonaid Project, the annual Investor Conference, various Holiday programs.

Erin became close friends with her neighbor Brandyn (as they are raising all young boys!) and Brandyn mentioned that she would love to get her organization, Lady Savant, to get out in the community and get more involved in philanthropic ventures. From there the Lady Savant Foundation took root. Erin organized more grass roots opportunities for the members such as volunteering in local soup kitchens and a Habitat For Humanity build day, before becoming more official as a registered 501c3.

Erin's passions are tennis and traveling. She graduated in 2000 from the University of Southern California with a BA in International Relations and an emphasis on Business Administration. She then went abroad after working at a dot.com, landing a dual Masters program in Europe. She graduated in 2002 with an MA from the University of Exeter (England) in European Studies, and an MA from the Universidad de Salamanca (Spain) in Latin American Studies (Maestria de Estudios Latinoamericanos). Her dissertation was focused on Political Risk Analysis.

Mariel Alvarado

Mariel is a mom, serial entrepreneur, best selling author, speaker, and inspiration junkie in love with the world of creativity and spirituality. She has been a student of the metaphysical book A Course In Miracles for over a decade and she practices Transcendental Meditation as taught by The David Lynch Foundation.

Over the past 10 years Mariel has built 7 successful businesses (6 of them with her BFF and partner Brandyn Randolph).

Her mission is to enjoy and celebrate life with her family which includes two tireless little boys and to connect women to their own creative and divine greatness. She does this by building brands, platforms and tools that support and encourage women to build successful businesses and lives.

Mariel loves fashion, art, yoga and is a trained wine enthusiast. She is a graduate of the University of Houston where she received a Bachelor of Science in Marketing and A Bachelor of Arts in Communication but she is constantly fueling her hustle by taking courses and learning from people who inspire her.

Brandyn Randolph

Brandyn is a mom, wife, co-founder of a female entrepreneurial club, best selling author, speaker, publisher, and a lover of connecting people. She is a graduate of Texas A&M with a Bachelor Degree in Communications and has a background in real estate management and recruitment. After having her second son she ventured out and spread her wings and became a serial entrepreneur. Over the past 5 years, she has had 5 successful businesses with her best friend and business partner, Mariel Alvarado. They have owned everything from a furniture business, wine business, and now currently publishing, networking, hosting retreats, and health and wellness.

Her mission is to help other women build successful businesses by connecting them to other amazing other women. She love watching others succeed and its her goal in life to help or support them in anyway she can. She also believes in the old saying, "It not always what you know, but it's WHO you know."

She is very passionate about learning new things and believes women should always keep their mind fresh and moving. She believes the best lessons to learn in life is to listen to others who have been there and done that whether it's about business or life in general. Her boys four boys (including husband) are the most important, family and friends.
