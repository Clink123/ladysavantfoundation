Collaborations

Lady Savant has worked with an assortment of local programs here in Bergen County, NJ. We have donated time to a local soup kitchen, had a build day with Habitat for Humanity and also worked with Valley Hospital's Home Care Program.

We have also collaborated with the Robin Hood Foundation providing toys for their Adopt a Family and Gift Drive programs.

In addition, we have worked with the Spring Lake Toys Foundation in various opportunities to assist with their ill children.
