Family #2: Single Mom Aurora, Daughter Sophia, Mathias, and Peyton (Dog)

Aurora and her children Mathias and Sophia were rescued by first responders this past Sunday morning (8/27/17) from waist-high water caused by Hurricane Harvey at their home at Houston TX. Fortunately they are now safe and dry, and living temporarily with friends.

However, everything is lost - home, car, clothes, all possessions large and small, toys and necessities.

Everyone who knows Aurora knows about her selfless qualities, particularly the love for her children and family, her determination and strength, and her generosity. We have no doubt that her character together with our help will enable Aurora, Mathias and Sophia to recover from this disaster and move forward.

We are reaching out to you to ask for your kind help and collect donations to support Aurora and her family so they can recover from their loss.

Any amount will be of help and please see their 'Essential Items' list on Amazon here.

The list includes essentials such as underwear, socks, kitchen items and more. Sophia in in second grade and wears a size 10 in clothing. Mathias is in 8th grade and wears an XL in kids or S in adults.

Cash is essential as well in this situation so please consider donating gift cards or monetary donations below.

If you prefer making donations directly to her please contact her at acastelan77@gmail.com
