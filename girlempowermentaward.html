The Lady Savant Foundation Girl Empowerment Award

This scholarship is to be awarded to an outstanding female leader who has made a significant and positive impact in their community. The award is to promote community service and empower tomorrow's female leaders.

Criteria:

  • Must be a high school female senior
  • Must have applied or been accepted to a 4 year college
  • Maintain a 3.0 GPA
  • Demonstrated outstanding community service and leadership abilities
  • Have completed or enrolled in at least 1 AP or IB class
  • Must submit a two paragraph essay and a picture of you doing your service project
