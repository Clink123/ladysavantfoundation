Mission for 2017

Empowering the Next Generation: EDUCATION

Our first year is almost complete! We can't believe how fast the year has gone, but check out what we've been up to. THANK YOU FOR ALL OF YOUR SUPPORT THROUGHOUT THE YEAR!

We are thrilled to announce the launch of the Lady Savant Foundation School in Cambodia. Construction is finished and students are slated to start Nov 1, 2017 in their new school. We are excited to move on to the next steps which are starting our Farmstead Capital Garden of Life, building out our new computer lab, and soccer/athletic field.

With your support, we were able to have three classrooms equivalent to US 6th-8th grades. In our school, we are proud to 'sponsor' 20 girl students in our Girls Be Ambitious program, which is an incentive program to keep girls safe, educated, and out of the human trafficking circles. Please check back soon for more updates!

In the US, we had to pivot away from our school building initiative and focus our efforts in Houston. Click here to see their stories. We are happy to announce almost all are back on their feet and living their busy daily lives.

Morgan Gorospe and Meghan Buchert have been added to our Board of Directors! A huge congrats to both women and we are excited to have them on board and feel grateful for lending their expertise to our team. Click here to see their bios.

We have been so busy out in the community this year! Check out all of the fun events we have been a part of here including our fundraising gala, back to school fashion show, screening of The Other F Word, and more.
